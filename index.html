About Me




I'm a deep learning enthusiast with a passion for research.

I have been interested in technology and the nature of mind since high school. My computer science studies were motivated by a desire to understand the nature of intelligence through the lens of machine learning. This led me to work in the areas of adversarial robustness and causal modeling. I like to keep up with the latest research in deep learning and causality, and I find great joy in reading and understanding challenging concepts in the scientific literature.

I hold a BSc. in computer science from Aarhus University (2017-2020) and an MSc. in computer science from the IT University of Copenhagen (2020-2022). Feel free to check out some of the projects I've worked on.
